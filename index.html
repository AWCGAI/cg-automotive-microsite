eBay Motors

eBay Garage Festival Activation

38k
visitors engaged over event weekend
4.7x
increase in platform visits during activation

We designed and delivered the eBay Garage — a multi-level experiential pop-up at one of the UK's largest outdoor automotive and lifestyle festivals, bringing eBay's vehicle parts and accessories category to life in a way that was tactile, social, and genuinely useful to enthusiasts. The structure became a navigational landmark at the event, drawing sustained dwell time and driving measurable traffic to the eBay Motors platform. It repositioned eBay as a destination for serious automotive buyers, not just casual browsers.
